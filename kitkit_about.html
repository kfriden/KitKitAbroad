KitKit's Travel Tips!

Transportation in the Netherlands can be a hassle at times, don't stress and get an OV chip card!

Getting an OV chipcard saves time so you can explore more! Simply purchase an anonymous chipcard (can find them being sold at airports, train stations, or newstands) and load your euros onto the card. Make sure at least 20 euros are on the card when using public transportation. You can find out more here

Must see places to visit in the Netherlands

Explore the best sites at Kinderdijk, a UNESCO heritage site, just outside of Rotterdam

Relax at Melief Bender and Biergarten, both with an impressive beer selection

Enjoy gorgeous paintings and art at the Rijksmuseum in the heart of Amsterdam

If you are a convention goer, the Jaarbeurs in Utrecht holds (one of my favorites) Dutch Comic Con every March and November. Get tickets here

A Little About Me

Hi there! My name is Kait, or you can call me by my online presence name KitKit. I am a travel blogger specializing in finding places to visit in the Netherlands, specifically the bigger cities. I always love exploring and trying new things and sharing my adventures with my friends and family. The reason I wound up making a travel site dedicated to the Netherlands is because I made a lot of friends over there and even found love there a few times. I first visited in 2017 making two trips, again in 2018 for two more trips, and this year in 2019 I am expected to make two more trips again. Everyone I have met there so far has shown me so many great places to see as well as to eat, and now I want to be able to share that with you! With my website, I hope to give helpful tips, list places sometimes off the beaten path, so to speak, as well as more must see touristy places. I hope I inspire young and old travelers to come visit this wonderful country and come see for yourself why I love it here so much! Proost!
